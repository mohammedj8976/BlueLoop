Rewards

Points that encourage real-world environmental action.

BlueLoop rewards verified reporting, evidence uploads, and campaign participation so people keep contributing to cleaner seas.

How points are earned

  1. Spot

    Find a pollution issue

    Notice plastic, oil traces, abandoned gear, or other coastal pollution.

  2. Submit

    Send a useful report

    Add location, description, and image evidence for review.

  3. Verify

    Report is checked

    BlueLoop reviews report quality and environmental relevance.

  4. Earn

    Receive points

    Verified action earns reward points and campaign recognition.

Reward examples

+50Clear pollution location report
+75Report with helpful image evidence
+120Campaign participation or cleanup support

Community impact

Rewards are not just points. They are a way to make care visible.

Every point represents someone paying attention to Bahrain's marine environment and helping partners understand where action is needed.

Rewards FAQ

Do all reports earn points?

Points are intended for useful, relevant, and clear reports that help BlueLoop understand pollution conditions.

Can campaign participation earn extra points?

Yes. Campaign participation can earn additional points because it supports coordinated community action.

Why does evidence matter?

Images help verify the report and make cleanup planning more accurate.
